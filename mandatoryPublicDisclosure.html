Maharishi Vidya Mandir (MVM), Orai is one of the leading CBSE schools in Orai and a part of the prestigious Maharishi Global Education Movement. Established in 1992, the school is spread across 2.5 acres and provides quality education from Nursery to Class XII. With modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, smart classrooms, library, laboratories, yoga and meditation facilities, MVM Orai focuses on the holistic development of every student through Consciousness Based Education.
